What does Saans mean?

Saans, is a Hindi (one of the languages from india) word, which simply means to breathe. In this day and age, with so much happening in our world, we can get caught up in the ups and downs, and sometimes forget to breathe.

A breath is so essential to being alive. You may have experienced learning how to breathe in a meditation or yoga, mindfulness or pilates class, and I am hoping you experienced a deep sensation connecting you to your heart, soul and body. And it is this connection we lose when we are anxious, uncertain, nervous, angry, sad. Sometimes, when we are filled with so many emotions, we can forget to breathe. A breath should be one that allows us to feel fully and be alive.

I chose this word for the practice as it symbolically represents many times in life when we just forget to breathe. I feel that in working through your life concerns and questions of life, and living, we can work together and learn to breathe a full breath, and connect to ourselves deeply.
